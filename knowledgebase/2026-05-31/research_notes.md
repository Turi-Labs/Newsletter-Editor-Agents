The law requires employers to keep their staff informed about when and how AI systems are being integrated into processes that could affect their roles or job security.

This policy change is significant as it sets a potential precedent for how other states or regions might regulate the deployment of AI in business settings. By ensuring that employees are aware of AI usage, the law aims to foster trust and mitigate concerns over worker displacement and fairness.
